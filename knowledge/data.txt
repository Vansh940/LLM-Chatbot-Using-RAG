Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem solving, perception, and language understanding.

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that allows computers to learn patterns from data instead of being explicitly programmed. Machine learning algorithms improve their performance as they are exposed to more data.

Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers. These neural networks are designed to simulate the structure and function of the human brain.

Neural networks are computing systems inspired by biological neural networks in the human brain. They consist of layers of interconnected nodes called neurons that process information.

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of artificial intelligence that focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.

A Large Language Model (LLM) is a type of AI model trained on massive amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like language. Examples include GPT models and other transformer-based architectures.

The Transformer architecture is a neural network architecture introduced in 2017. It uses a mechanism called attention to process sequences of data and has become the foundation for most modern language models.

Python is one of the most widely used programming languages in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science because of its simplicity and extensive ecosystem of libraries.

Data science is a multidisciplinary field that combines statistics, computer science, and domain knowledge to extract insights from data.

The capital of Japan is Tokyo. Tokyo is one of the largest metropolitan areas in the world and serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of Japan.

The capital of France is Paris. Paris is known for its history, culture, architecture, and landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower.

The capital of India is New Delhi. New Delhi is the seat of all three branches of the Indian government.

The capital of the United States is Washington, D.C. It serves as the political center of the country.

Geopolitics is the study of how geography, economics, and political power influence international relations between countries.

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns, primarily caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels.

Renewable energy is energy generated from natural sources that are replenished constantly, such as solar energy, wind energy, and hydroelectric power.

The Internet is a global network of interconnected computers that communicate with each other using standardized protocols.

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, or damage.

Databases are systems designed to store, organize, and retrieve large amounts of information efficiently.

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services such as servers, storage, databases, networking, and software over the internet.

Robotics is a field of engineering and computer science that focuses on designing and building robots capable of performing tasks automatically.

Space exploration is the investigation of outer space using telescopes, satellites, spacecraft, and human space missions.

The Solar System consists of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, including eight planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.
